About us

What is Epsilon and What are our goals?

Epsilon has a fully segregated discord for people who only want to see certain things and not to be spammed or pinged with irrelevant stuff.

Epsilon is a community of people who have similar interests.

Epsilon wants to make and have the funding for game servers.

Epsilon is looking to provide a platform for others to play games together.

Epsilon follows the rules of:

  1. Epsilon Community's Charter.
  2. Discord’s Code of Conduct.
  3. Squad’s Code of Conduct and Squad’s Server Policies for Administration and Squad’s Server License Policy.
